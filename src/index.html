Vision

The Future of Eatonville

Let's celebrate our unique town's history and culture.

Action: I will work to retain the unique characteristics that make our town a great place to live, work, and visit while charting a common-sense course for Eatonville's future prosperity.

Cooperation

On behalf of all residents, the council must put aside petty bickering and self-serving grandstanding. Disagreements naturally arise, but discourse should remain civil.

Action: I will maintain good relations with the mayor, town employees, and my fellow council members.

Crime & Public Safety

The first duty of government is to protect its citizens.

Action: I will strongly support our police department and their efforts to keep our town safe.

Private Property Rights

It's obvious but worth repeating - your property is yours. Town building regulations should be clear and easy to understand, and permit approvals should be made quickly without overburdening property owners

Action: : I will work diligently to remove unnecessary regulation and streamline town processes.

Pro-Business

Eatonville businesses are an asset to our town.

Action: I will encourage local businesses and promote a pro-business climate to help them succeed.

Taxes and Fees

Necessary services cost money, and taxes must be levied to pay for them.

Action: I will scrutinize unnecessary "nice to have" projects and, in most cases, not support funding them with tax dollars.
